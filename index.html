Critical Blocker: Onboarding "Hidden" Failures

While the API's reasoning and multimodal capabilities (like Nano Banana Pro) are seen as superior, developers are being "bounced" at the front door. The data shows that even when a developer is willing to pay, they encounter 404 "Model Not Found" or 429 "Resource Exhausted" errors that are poorly explained in the documentation.

Developer Perception:
"Many developers perceive this as the API being 'broken' rather than a quota/billing synchronization issue, leading them to abandon the platform."
